Rigetti is developing a modular chiplet architecture for superconducting quantum processors. The 36-qubit modular system was released in mid-2025, with a 100+ qubit system expected by end of year. Rigetti's approach focuses on scalable multi-chip integration, connecting smaller chiplets to build larger processors without sacrificing coherence.
