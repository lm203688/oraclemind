X-energy Xe-100 (SMR)

smr
The Xe-100 is a modular HTGR using TRISO fuel in a pebble bed design. Each 80 MWe module can be combined into multi-module plants. The high outlet temperature (750°C) enables industrial process heat applications beyond electricity generation. Dow Chemical plans to install Xe-100 at their Texas facility for industrial heat and power.
