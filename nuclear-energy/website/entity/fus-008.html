Direct-Drive ICF

fusion
Direct-drive ICF illuminates the fuel capsule directly with laser beams, avoiding the energy loss of the hohlraum used in NIF's indirect-drive approach. This could achieve higher coupling efficiency (more laser energy reaches the fuel) but requires more uniform laser illumination. The University of Rochester's OMEGA laser and future kJ-class lasers are testing this approach.
