Copenhagen Atomics Molten Salt Reactor

smr
Copenhagen Atomics is developing a thorium-fueled molten salt reactor designed for factory mass production. The liquid fuel eliminates fuel fabrication costs and allows online refueling. Thorium is more abundant than uranium and produces less long-lived waste. Copenhagen Atomics is building and testing prototypes in Copenhagen and aims for first deployment by 2028.
